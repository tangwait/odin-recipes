Vietnamese Sandwiches (Banh Mi)

A staple Vietnamese dish that is eaten everyday using fresh herbs and vegetables. The combination of vegetables and your choice of meat creates a healthy and filling sandwich that will leave you invigorated.

Ingredients

  • meat (chicken thigh/breast, pork breast)
  • seasonings (sesame oil, honey, hoisin sauce, soy sauce, sugar, rice wine vineger
  • garlic
  • carrots
  • cucumber
  • cilantro
  • green chili peppers
  • baguette
  • Maggi Seasoning

Steps

Veggie prep

  1. Peel and mince garlic
  2. Peel and slice carrots into matchsticks
  3. Peel and slice cucumber into ribbons
  4. Slice up chili peppers
  5. Mix sugar with rice wine vinegar, add 1 tbsp salt, and a cup of hot water. Then submerge the sliced carrots for an hour

Cooking the meat

  1. Chop the meat into bite sized chunks
  2. Mix the minced garlic with sesame oil, honey, hoisin sauce, and soy sauce
  3. Pour marinade over the chicken and stick it in the fridge for about 15 minutes

Putting it together

After the meat is cooked, let it cool and drain the carrots. Afterwards slice the baguette in half and remove the filling. Spread mayonnaise and sprinkle some Maggi Seasoning on the bread. Arrange your meat on half the bread and top it off with carrots, cucumber slices, cilantro, and chilis.
